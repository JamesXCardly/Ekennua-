Principal Consulting Partner

Osamede Okhomina has over 25 years experience in developing oil and gas projects all across the African region. From initiating and managing Joint Ventures with oil majors (Exxon and Chevron), to resolving complex social issues for IOCs (Shell). Osamede has also been involved in bringing several assets from exploration to production and has secured and deployed over $500m into African projects. Osamede has also been CEO of both private and public oil companies. Aside from in Europe and North America, in Africa, has worked in Angola, Gabon, Equatorial Guinea, South Africa, Egypt, Libya and mostly in Nigeria. More recently, he has provided strategic adviser and assisted with capital raising for companies in diverse sectors including mining and fintech. He also advises on sovereign capital raising to African Governments and structured financing products to private and public sector corporations.
